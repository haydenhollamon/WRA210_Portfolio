overview

The website (that you are currently on) was a semester long project to create a portfolio in order to begin to develop a personal design process while learning the fundementals of HTML and CSS.

The goal was to create a fun and immersive portfolio to display work, while maintaining and communicating personal style.

process

This site came to fruition in 5 manin steps; gathering inspiration, sketching, wireframing, mockups and finally building the website. I found inspiration through three main mediums: other websites, magazines, and things around me. I took a lot of screenshots of websites I liked and noted things about them that I liked.

planning

Next I started quickly sketching what I may want my website to look like, specifically keeping in minda layout, and the pages that I want to include in my website, but also thinking about creative choices that could be incorproated design, such us a simple animated character or a a portfolio that spills my projects all down the page. This was a short process, meant to get my ideas on paper without too much thought with the intent of discovering something that I hadnt originally thought of.

I then made a more formal wireframe on adobe illustrator to get a sense of the final version of the website that I was heading towards. I ended coming up with two directions that I saw myself going in, one a portfolio spilling all of my projects down the page, and another more traditional layout.

style tile

next I developed a style tile for the two websites (heads up I ended up changing the entire thing). I thought that I wanted something very traditional, minimal, and a little sophisticated. I wanted to include circular elements to make the site a little smoother than It was feeling in the wireframes, but I didn't really know where else to go in terms of styling. I decided while making the mockups, shown bellow that I wanted to be a little more fun and trendy than I originally intended with this style tile, because that is a good reflection of my personality

mockup

Finally, before diving into the build I made the mockups which is where I really honed down on my ideas. I started thinking about conrinuity in the design.
